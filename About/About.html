About This Website

Welcome to Discover Cebu!

This website was created to showcase the beauty, culture, and adventure that Cebu, Philippines, has to offer. Known as the “Queen City of the South,” Cebu is home to some of the country’s most iconic landmarks, stunning natural wonders, and unforgettable experiences.

Our Purpose

Traveling can sometimes feel overwhelming, especially when planning where to go and what to see. That’s why this website focuses on the Top 5 Tourist Spots in Cebu—a simple, curated guide for travelers who want to make the most out of their trip. From historic sites like Magellan’s Cross to natural treasures like Kawasan Falls, our goal is to help you explore Cebu with ease and excitement.

What You’ll Find Here

On this site, you’ll discover:


  • Background information about Cebu and its culture.
  • Details and history of the 5 most popular attractions.
  • Practical directions and tips for visiting each location.
  • A gallery of photos to give you a glimpse of Cebu’s charm.
